Coneshare is an open-source platform for security-conscious teams to securely share documents and datarooms, track engagement, and automate follow-up workflows in real time.

[Get Started](https://github.com/coneshare/coneshare-compose) · [Docs](https://docs.coneshare.com/en/) · [Live Demo](https://www.coneshare.com/demo) · [Roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/coneshare/projects/2/) · [Forum](https://github.com/orgs/coneshare/discussions)

⭐ If Coneshare is useful for your team, please star our repositories.

## Core Features

- **Self-hosted by design**: Keep sensitive files and activity data on your infrastructure.
- **Secure sharing controls**: Password, expiration, email verification, and download restrictions.
- **Dataroom-ready workflows**: Granular visibility and permissions for deal collaboration.
- **Real-time engagement analytics**: Track document and dataroom activity to understand buyer/investor behavior.
- **Workflow automation**: Trigger Slack and webhook actions from engagement events.
- **Reliable delivery operations**: Use delivery logs with retry and replay support.
- **Dynamic watermarking**: Protect sensitive documents with viewer-aware watermarking.
